What is Sexual Wellness?

When you have a headache, you go to a physician. When you have sexual issues, you go to a Sexual Wellness Expert.

Half of the world’s population experiences sexual health issues at some point in their life, but finding reliable and secure sex therapists is always a challenge.

You can find verified, curated Sexual Wellness Experts on Thriive.

Sexual Wellness Expert (Sex Therapists) vs. Sexologists?

The two sometimes overlap, but aren’t the same. The key difference between a Sexologist and Sexual Wellness Expert is that Sexologists can prescribe medication, since they mostly deal with the physical issues. Sex Therapists deal with emotional, psychological, and mental aspect of sexual health, and provide counselling.
